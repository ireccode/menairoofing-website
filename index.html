WHAT DO WE OFFER?

Comprehensive Roofing Repairs and Restoration Services

Fast & Reliable Roof Leak Repairs

Protect your home from costly water damage with our quick and effective roof leak repairs. We locate and fix leaks in tile, metal, and Colorbond roofs, delivering durable solutions that stand up to local weather conditions.

Storm & Hail Damage Roof Repairs

From cracked tiles to lifted metal sheets, storm damage can compromise your home's safety. Our storm damage repair team responds fast, restoring your roof's strength and preventing further damage. We also help homeowners with insurance claims for a stress-free repair process.

Broken Tile & Ridge Capping Repairs

Loose ridge capping and cracked tiles can quickly lead to leaks. Our roofing repair experts replace damaged tiles and re-bed or re-point ridge caps using premium materials, keeping your roof secure and looking its best throughout the Sutherland Shire.

POPULAR QUESTIONS

Get Answers to Your Questions

What types of roof repairs do you handle?

Based in Menai and servicing the entire Sutherland Shire, NSW, we handle all types of roof repairs, including tile, metal, Colorbond, and slate. From leaking roofs to storm damage and general wear, our expert roofers provide fast, reliable repairs that last.

How quickly can you attend to an emergency roof leak?

We offer same-day emergency roof leak repairs across the Sutherland Shire. Our team can respond quickly to stop water damage and protect your property — even during severe weather events.

Do you provide free roof inspections and quotes?

Yes, we provide free roof inspections and no-obligation quotes throughout the Sutherland Shire, NSW. We’ll inspect your roof, identify issues, and recommend cost-effective repair solutions tailored to your needs.

Are your roofing repairs covered by warranty?

All our roof repairs are backed by a workmanship guarantee, and we use high-quality roofing materials that are backed by manufacturer warranties. This ensures your repairs last for years to come.

Can you help with insurance claims for roof storm damage?

Yes. We regularly assist homeowners with roof insurance claims after storm or hail damage. Our team can provide detailed inspection reports, photos, and written quotes to help you get your claim approved quickly and smoothly.
